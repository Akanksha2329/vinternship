## About This Vlog

In this video, I share my experience as a participant in the VLED Lab Vinternship under IIT Ropar.

I begin by introducing myself and explaining how I got selected for the internship. I then describe the overall structure of the Vinternship, including how it is organized and what is expected from participants.

The vlog also includes an overview of the Vibe learning platform, where we complete structured modules as part of the program.

## What the Video Covers

- How I got selected for the VLED Lab Vinternship  
- Structure and format of the internship  
- Learning through the Vibe platform  
- Case studies and demonstration of one program  
- Internship activities and health points concept 
- My personal reflections and experience throughout the journey  

Through this vlog, I aim to give viewers a clear understanding of how the Vinternship works and what the learning experience feels like from a participant’s perspective.

This journey has helped me grow not only technically but also in terms of consistency, discipline, and resilience while balancing academic responsibilities.
